Support

Attending a physical location can be a barrier for many students who lack transportation, experience anxiety around hospitals, or face other accessibility challenges. The first five resources are online, while the remaining are in-person and location dependent for students with the means to attend.

Please read the purposes behind the listed tabs, if one fits your description, click on the box to view the attached phone number . You can view more information about each resource by clicking the name.

Pregnancy Help Online

Offers free, confidential help and information about pregnancy signs, symptoms and all options.

AyanMaternity HealthCare Support

Their Multilingual Services program ensures that all families can have equal access maternity information through providing services in Somali, Arabic, Swahili, Amharic, and English.

National Abortion Federation Hotline

The largest national, toll-free, multi-lingual Hotline for abortion referrals and financial assistance in the U.S. and Canada.

Pregnancy Decision Line

 This line acts as a pregnancy and abortion information helpline. Whether you are considering abortion, adoption or parenting; they provide caring, confidential support.
